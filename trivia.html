Trivia Club Robbinsville

Mission Statement

The purpose of this club is to teach students information in an informative and fun
way. The trivia questions can range from appropriate school topics such as: History, Math,
Science, Drivers Ed, Health, and English. Topics can also be from current news, politics,
and other subject matter. All the students will be given the opportunity to create their own trivia questions
and present and quiz the rest of the members. This will develop their public speaking and team
building skills. There will be a variety of ways to present the trivia questions using various game
show formats. Some of the possible games will include: Jeopardy, Family Feud, Are you smarter than a
fifth grader, kahoot, etc. Overall, the goal of this club is to entertain high school students, while passing on
knowledge to them. Not only will they acquire knowledge, but will also learn how to work with
others and speak out in public.
